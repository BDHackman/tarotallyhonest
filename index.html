About Me

I've spent a long time performing. Not just on stage, but as a version of myself built for other people's comfort. Being a gay man taught me how to read a room before I ever touched a deck of cards, because for years, reading a room was how I protected myself. But as I grew older, I realized this ability was more than survival instinct. The first time I put a deck of cards in my hands, I picked up on information I had no way of knowing otherwise. That's when I learned I'm an intuitive.

It's taken me a long time to just be me. Real. A little loud. A little messy. That's the same honesty I bring to your reading. I'm not interested in performing for you, so I'll tell you what the cards say, straight up. And since I'm asking for your honesty, here's mine first:

I'm a blue-eyed, born and raised Minnesotan with Norwegian heritage (Skol), a Scorpio sun with a Libra moon and rising, donor conceived (thirty-plus half-siblings and counting), dog dad, uncle, brother, and married to my husband since 2017. None of that is the interesting part. The interesting part is what it took to get from performing every day to building a life, and now a practice, where I don't have to anymore.

Consider the library open.

— Bryce Hackman

My Method

"The cards tell me what's really going on. I tell you what you need to hear."

What happens in one of my readings is honest human energy passing between people. The cards cut through the rest of it: the facades, the stories we tell ourselves, all the embellishment humans love to layer on top of what's real. They read the energy and pick up the truth underneath. I'm the one channeling that and translating it for you.

I'm not a psychic, and I'm not a fortune teller. I'm not a medium, and I don't talk to the dead. I'm not reading your body language and calling it magic, and I'm not promising to predict anything. What I am is intuitive and genuinely gifted at picking up on energy. Tarot is just the instrument that brings my gift into focus.

What You'll Experience

  • No smoke and mirrors. Just you, me, and a deck that cares about the truth.
  • Together, we'll sit with what comes up and unpack what it means for your situation.
  • Expect a conversation, not a monologue. The best readings go back and forth, not one direction.
